How to Care for Adopted Pets

Here are some tips on how to care for your adopted pet:

  • Provide a comfortable and safe living environment for your pet.
  • Feed your pet a healthy and balanced diet.
  • Make sure your pet gets enough exercise and playtime.
  • Take your pet to the vet for regular checkups and vaccinations.
  • Give your pet plenty of love and attention.
